# Sales-Analysis-of-a-Clothing-Business-Using-Python
This project analyzes clothing business sales data using Python to uncover key insights on revenue, product performance, customer demand, and seasonal trends. Through data cleaning, exploratory analysis, and visualizations, it supports data-driven decisions for inventory planning, marketing strategy, and overall business growth.

**Objectives**

1) Analyze overall sales performance of the clothing business
2) Identify top-selling categories, products, and regions
3) Understand seasonal and monthly sales trends
4) Perform gender-wise and category-wise revenue analysis
5) Provide actionable insights for business growth

   **Tools & Technologies Used**
1) Python
2) Pandas – Data cleaning and manipulation
3) Matplotlib – Data visualization
4) NumPy – Numerical operations
5) Google : colab

**Analysis Performed**

1) Data Cleaning & Preprocessing
2) Handling missing values
3) Date formatting and feature extraction
4) Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
5) Total sales and quantity sold
6) Category-wise and gender-wise sales distribution
7) State-wise revenue contribution
8) Time Series Analysis
9) Monthly sales trends
10) Identification of peak sales periods
11) Revenue contribution by product


**Conclusion**
This project demonstrates how Python can be effectively used to analyze retail sales data and generate valuable business insights.
The analysis supports better decision-making in areas such as stock optimization, marketing focus, and regional strategy planning.
